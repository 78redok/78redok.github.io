Cremá: Design Story


Many students, working professionals and everyone in between love to get a cup of brew to get their mornings off to a right start. Among those individuals, there are many who additionally enjoy the ambiance of a café to be productive. While the desire to fulfill this yearning may be strong, at times it is met with an abrupt cessation as they realize that there are no more seats available at their favorite cafés; much often too late. This can be a troublesome occurrence at day break because the rest of their day can be altered adversely. Our team, beanleaf, decided to address this problem through our Informatics 132 project by conducting user research to develop an application that will circumvent or mitigate this problem.
